Overview

Purpose

Rafting is a recreational outdoor adventure activity specially organizes in river which offers adventure thrill, rapids, fun and excitement utilizing a raft.
White water rafting is going popular water adventure between thrill seeking traveler.

Audience

Curious about rafting? We welcome any one from first-time thrill seekers, to seasoned river rats. This activity as an adventure sport has become popular since the 1950s, if not earlier, evolving from individuals paddling 10 feet (3.0 m) to 14 feet (4.3 m) rafts with double-bladed paddles or oars to multi-person rafts propelled by single-bladed paddles and steered by a person at the stern, or by the use of oars.

What ages are able to go water rafting?

A white water rafting trip with Smoky Mountain Outdoors is suitable for nearly all age groups, and absolutely no prior experience is required!
Kids who are at least eight years old or 70 pounds will love our exciting adventures through nature all while enjoying the splashing waters with trained professional guides. Our staff is always happy to help out all ages and experience levels!

How safe is it really?

All our staff is regularly certified on techniches and situations to make sure the activity is safe for the whole family.

All of our professional guides are certified in Standard First Aid, and every raft is furnished with a waterproof first aid kit. Every guide participates in a demanding training program that requires that they take at least 15 to 20 trips down the river before qualifying to lead a commercial trip.
In addition, prior to each trip, every group is provided a 20-minute safety talk to prepare families for a safe and enjoyable rafting trip. Our safety standards are some of the highest in the industry, so your family can simply focus on having fun!

Is white water rafting only a sunny day, summer activity?

Unlike most indoor activities, white water rafting is an adeventure that many can take in either the sunny summer days or wet winter months.
Unlike many of the outdoor attractions in the region, white water rafting in (Enter your hometown here) is a perfect rainy-day activity!
After all, getting wet is part of the family adventure! Of course, we may delay a trip for a few minutes if a storm needs to pass, but otherwise, it is business as usual on the raging waters. Regardless of the weather, be sure to bring along a towel and dry clothes so that you can change immediately following your trip.
